Sparkle Sweethearts

Decription of Delious Desert

To suprise a love one during valentines day try to make this delious Sparkle Sweetheart. With cruchy sugar on the outside to a creamy vanilla almond flavor on the inside, your loved one's will find this contrast pleasing and be waiting till Valentine's Day comes again. You could put whatever chocolate you want in the middle of the sweetheart, but I would reconomend the clasic flavor of cocoa. And don't worry if you won't able able to eat all of these cookies in one go, just wrap the batch and pop them in the freezer, and they will be fresh even month after. Also those making it last minute making these cookies is relativily quick so no worries. This recipe will only requries about 30 - 60 minutes of cookie dough chilling.

Ingredients Needed

  • 3 CUPS ALL PURPOSE FLOWERR (spooned and leveled)
  • 1 AND 1/2 TABLE SPOONS BAKING POWDER
  • 1/2 TEASPOONS SALT
  • 1 CUP UNSALTED BUTTER (room temperature)
  • 2 OUNCES BLOCK CREAM CHEESE (softened to room temperaturre)
  • 1 CUP GRANULATED SUGAR
  • 1 LARGE EGG (room temperature)
  • 2 TEASPOONS PURE VANILLA EXTRACT
  • 1/2 TEASPOON ALMOND EXTRACT
  • 3/4 CUP SANDING SUGAR (pink, red, or white)
  • 32 CHOCOLATE HEARTS

Steps

  1. Whisk the flour, baking powder, and salt together until combined.
  2. In a large bowl using a hand-held or stand mixerr fitted with a paddle attachment, beat the butter and cream cheese together on high speed until relatively smooth, about 2 minutes. Add the granulated sugar and beat until combined and crreamy. Add egg, vanilla extract, and almond extract and beat on high speed about 1 minute
  3. Add dry and ingredients to wet on a low speed. Then cover the dough tightly with aluminum foil, and chill for about 30 to 60 minutes.
  4. Prehat the oven to 350 degress Farenheight.
  5. Roll balls of cookie dough and about 1 tablespoon per cookie. Also roll each of the balls in the sander sujar to coat over the dough. Also make sure to use the back of a measuring cup and press down on each of the dough balls, to flaten. Then back the cookies for about 12-13 until the edges are lightly brown
  6. Remove cookies from oven and let them cool for 5 minutes. Then press a chocolate heart into each cookie, and put the backing sheeting into the fridge do the cookie doesn't melt the chocolate heart
  7. Then you are done enjoy!
